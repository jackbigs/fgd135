THE NEW FEDERALIST PARTY

“A Restoration of the American Idea”

The New Federalist Party does not seek to reinvent America, but to remember it. We believe in a government that acts with intellectual rigor, moral clarity, and a profound sense of duty.

I. PREAMBLE: THE CALL TO SERVICE

We reject the politics of the loudest voice in favor of the politics of the best idea. Our mission is simple: to ensure that the federal government remains a "City on a Hill"—a beacon of competence, justice, and unyielding strength.

II. DOMESTIC POLICY: ARCHITECTURE OF OPPORTUNITY

1. Education: The Great Equalizer

  • The Foundation: Implementation of universal, federally-funded Pre-K.
  • The Bartlet Guarantee: No student admitted to a public university is turned away due to inability to pay.
  • National Teacher Corps: Treating educators as national security assets.

2. Justice and the Civil Covenant

  • The Living Constitution: Appointing "The Best and the Brightest"—jurists who prioritize individual liberties.
  • The John Lewis Mandate: Comprehensive federal voting rights protections.
  • Rational Safety: Universal background checks for all firearm transfers.

III. ECONOMIC POLICY: FISCAL STEWARDSHIP

3. The "Grand Bargain"

  • A Century of Solvency: Securing the Social Security Trust Fund for the next 75 years.
  • The Fair Share Tax Code: Eliminating loopholes and taxing capital gains to reflect the value of labor.

IV. NATIONAL DEFENSE: THE SHIELD OF LIBERTY

4. Technological Primacy

  • The Digital Frontier: Pivoting to AI-driven defense and elite cyber-warfare.
  • The Proportional Plus Doctrine: A military capacity so advanced it serves as an absolute deterrent.

5. The Veteran Covenant

A total overhaul of the VA system, providing "concierge-level" healthcare and mental health services.

V. FOREIGN POLICY: THE BARTLET DOCTRINE

The United States has a moral obligation to prevent genocide. We lead through multilateral diplomacy first, but we do not stand by while the innocent are slaughtered.

VI. CONCLUSION: WHAT’S NEXT?

Decisions are made by those who show up. It’s time to get to work.
