About

The writer
behind the work

Born and raised in or near Salt Lake City, Utah. They baptized me but it certainly didn't stick. From there I've traveled the country a good bit. Made it across the Atlantic a few times in a burst of fabulous luck and happenstance and saw most of Western Europe. I'm currently awaiting sponsorship to Asia.

I've been doodling with words my whole life. It's later in my episode that I'm actually producing. Here's a sampling. I also spent a decade-plus shooting indie rock concerts — those photos will be posted as I get them scanned and organized.

I'm a proud, if not terribly outspoken, atheist. The Pacific Northwest became my home just before Covid. I very much doubt I'll leave.

The name fgd135 is a signal filter from a film. Designed to pass only the verified transmission and block everything else. It works until it doesn't. This site operates on the same principle.
