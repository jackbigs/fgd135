About

The writer
behind the work

Born and raised in or near Salt Lake City, Utah. They baptized me but it certainly didn't stick. From there I've traveled the country a good bit. Made it across the Atlantic a few times in a burst of fabulous luck and happenstance and saw most of Western Europe. I'm currently awaiting sponsorship to Asia :).

I've been doodling with words my whole life. It's later in my episode that I'm actually producing. Here's a sampling of my writing. Also, photos from my travels and music obsession. I spent a decade plus shooting indie rock concerts. Those photos will be posted as I get them scanned and organized.

I'm a proud, but not terribly outspoken atheist. The Pacific Northwest became my home just before Covid. I very much doubt I'll leave.

The name fgd135 is from Dr. Strangelove — a code on the CRM114 Discriminator, a device that filters out all but verified signal. It works until it doesn't. This site operates on a similar principle.
